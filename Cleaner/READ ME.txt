HOW TO INSTALL:

Drag the "Cleaner" - folder into the "datapacks" - folder of your world.

INGAME:

First activate cheats in your world.
When that's done type following Commands in the Chat:
1. /datapack enable "file/Cleaner"
2. /reload

RUN THE CLEANER:

Position yourself in the SOUTH WEST corner of the chunk, which will be the center.
To see the separated chunks press F3+G 
Press F3 again to see in wich direction you are facing (at the left side FACING: ... )

Now you can type the command in the chat:

/function game:function/cleaner
